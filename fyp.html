Project Overview

Designed to bridge the gap between complex legal jargon and everyday understanding. By leveraging artificial intelligence, this system processes custom legal datasets to provide accurate, context-aware answers to user queries.

Why We Built It

"Legal terminology is often inaccessible to the average person, creating a barrier to basic legal understanding. We developed this AI Assistant as our Final Year Project to democratize legal information. By training NLP models specifically on legal datasets, we aimed to build a reliable tool that could interpret plain-language questions and provide accurate, hallucination-free guidance, simulating a consultation with a real lawyer."

Architecture & Features

Django Backend

Structured a secure and scalable backend to manage API requests, user sessions, and database interactions efficiently.

Data Preprocessing

Handled large volumes of raw legal text, cleaning and structuring it into custom datasets for machine learning training.

AI Integration

Implemented NLP models to understand user intent and fetch the most legally sound and relevant responses without hallucinations.
